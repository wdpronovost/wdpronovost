WHY IT EXISTS

I was building with AI agents and needed a logo. What I had was an AI-generated PNG at some unknown resolution — and raster marks fall apart at the sizes a real site needs. I wanted to fix that from my phone, in the moment, without opening a design tool. So I built Skalable.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. 1ImportDrop in a photo, screenshot, or logo — PNG, JPEG, HEIC, WEBP.
  2. 2TraceSkalable reproduces the mark faithfully — colors, gradients, and detail kept intact.
  3. 3ExportOut comes a clean SVG you can scale, recolor, and reuse forever.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

  • Fidelity over shortcuts
  • Any messy source in
  • Resolution-independent out
  • Gradients preserved
  • Simplify only when you choose
  • No desktop, no design tool
